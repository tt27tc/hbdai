Dearest Pretty-ta

Dost thou graciously accept
this humble token of mine,

as this day
be the twenty-third
of March

in the year of our Lord
two thousand and twenty-five?

Are you listening to it?

Wholeheartedly,


A letter for your 26th

Many are the friends I see
yet in my sight
No friend like thee
I know that friendship
like ours lasts
Not because I don't have a choice
but because in us
I trust

And by the time we age
and greet each other
candles in line of 8
or even in the heaven gates...
may our bond still remain
But now we're young still
skin glowing and smiling in grace
Let us cherish the moment
and keep stacking new memories
Happy, happy birthday.

Thank you for every little thing.
Though not by blood,
you're my ate
one that I didn't know
I so dearly needed.
My first friend
My first roommate
And my first inspiration as a kid
The most pretty tita ever...
by face...
and by heart.
Godspeed to your journeys ahead.


I love you, Tita Ai!
